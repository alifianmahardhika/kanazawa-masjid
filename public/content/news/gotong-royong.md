Assalamu'alaikum warahmatullahi wabarakatuh,

We are calling all community members to join our monthly **Community Mosque Cleaning Day** (Gotong Royong). Keeping the mosque clean is a form of worship, and it is a collective responsibility that brings our community closer together.

## When & Where

- **Date:** Last Saturday of each month
- **Time:** 09:00 – 12:00
- **Location:** Kanazawa Umar bin Al-Khattab Mosque

## What We Will Do

During the cleaning day, we will:

- Clean and mop the main prayer hall
- Clean and organize the wudu (ablution) area
- Tidy up the entrance and shoe racks
- Clean windows and doors
- Organize the mosque library
- General maintenance check

## What to Bring

- Work clothes (comfortable, modest)
- Cleaning gloves (optional, we have extras)
- Your energy and positive spirit!

Cleaning supplies will be provided by the mosque.

## Why Participate?

The Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him) said:

> "Cleanliness is half of faith." *(Sahih Muslim)*

The mosque is our shared home for worship. By maintaining its cleanliness, we honor the sanctity of the space and create a welcoming environment for all worshippers, including newcomers to our community.

## After Cleaning

We will share a light meal together after the cleaning session. A great opportunity to get to know your fellow community members!

## Contact

If you have questions or want to contribute supplies, please contact us at **info@kanazawamasjid.jp**.

Jazakumullahu khairan for your participation and support.

*Mosque Committee*
