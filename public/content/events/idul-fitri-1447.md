Assalamu'alaikum warahmatullahi wabarakatuh,

Taqabbalallahu minna wa minkum — May Allah accept from us and from you.

The Kanazawa Umar bin Al-Khattab Mosque is pleased to announce the **Eid al-Fitr 1447H** prayer and community celebration.

## Eid Prayer

- **Date:** Monday, March 30, 2026 (subject to moon sighting confirmation)
- **Time:** 07:30 (gates open at 07:00)
- **Location:** Main prayer hall, Kanazawa Umar bin Al-Khattab Mosque
- **Imam & Khatib:** Ustadz Ahmad Fauzi

Please bring your own prayer mat if possible. Women and children are very welcome.

## Community Gathering

After the Eid prayer and khutbah, we will hold a community open house:

- **Time:** 09:00 onwards
- **Activities:**
  - Salam-salaman (greeting and handshaking)
  - Communal breakfast (sumbangan makanan sangat dihargai / food contributions welcome)
  - Activities for children
  - Photo session

## Dress Code

Please wear your best attire (Eid clothing). Traditional dress from any country is welcome and encouraged!

## Parking & Access

Limited parking is available near the mosque. We encourage using public transport:
- **Bus:** Routes 50, 52, 60 stop nearby
- **Cycling:** Bike parking available

## Food Contributions

If you would like to contribute food or snacks for the community gathering, please contact us by **March 27** at **info@kanazawamasjid.jp** so we can coordinate.

---

We look forward to celebrating this blessed occasion together as one community. Eid Mubarak!
