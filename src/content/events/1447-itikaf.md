## About This Event

Alhamdulillah, as we enter the blessed final nights of Ramadan 1447H, the Kanazawa Umar bin Al-Khattab Mosque warmly invites all brothers to observe I'tikaf — a spiritual retreat within the masjid, dedicated to worship, Quran recitation, and the remembrance of Allah.

- **Date:** Saturday, 14 March 2026
- **Location:** Kanazawa Masjid, Kanazawa, Ishikawa Prefecture
- **Admission:** Free

### Program

1. Iftar and Maghrib prayer in congregation
2. Light dinner and community gathering
3. Isha and Tarawih prayer in congregation (20 raka'at, 1 Juz — Para 8, without Witr)
4. Full Quran recitation (30 Juz)
5. Laylatul Qadr explanation for children

## Qiyamul Lail

Due to limited space, the overnight program is open to brothers only. We will perform Qiyamul Lail (night prayer) together before Suhoor.

- **Date:** Sunday, 15 March 2026
- **Time:** 02:45 – 03:45
- **Location:** Kanazawa Masjid

If you would like to order Suhoor, please contact us through our community group.

## What to Bring

- Prayer mat and personal worship items
- Quran / Mushaf
- Sleeping bag or blanket (for those staying overnight)
- Light snacks

## Contact

For more information, please visit our contact page or reach out via the community WhatsApp group.

May Allah grant us the blessings of Laylatul Qadr and accept our deeds. Ameen.
