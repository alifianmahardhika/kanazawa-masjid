Assalamu'alaikum warahmatullahi wabarakatuh,

The Kanazawa Umar bin Al-Khattab Mosque committee is pleased to announce the complete schedule for the holy month of Ramadan 1447H. We warmly invite all members of the Muslim community in Kanazawa and surrounding areas to join us in worship and community activities.

## Tarawih Prayers

Tarawih prayers will be held every night of Ramadan starting from the first night:

- **Time:** After Isha prayer (approximately 19:30)
- **Location:** Main prayer hall
- **Imam:** Ustadz Ilyas Maulana & Ahmed
- **Rakaat:** 20 rakaat tarawih + 3 rakaat witr

## Iftar (Breaking Fast) Program

The mosque will organize a community iftar every **Saturday** throughout Ramadan.

- **Time:** 30 minutes before Maghrib
- **Contribution:** Donations and food contributions are welcome
- **Registration:** Please inform the committee by Thursday to help with preparation

## Eid al-Fitr

Details regarding the Eid al-Fitr prayer time and location will be announced in a separate notice. Please check our news section for updates.
