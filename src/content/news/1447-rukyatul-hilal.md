Assalamu Alaikum wa Rahmatullahi wa Barakatuh,

![Shawwal 1447H Meeting](/images/news/1447-Shawal-Meeting.jpeg)

**R.H.C.J. (Ruyat Hilal Committee Japan)** requests all Masjids, Musallahs and Muslim organizations in Japan to **try to see Hilal (the new moon) on Thursday 29 Ramadan 1447 A.H. (19 Mar. 2026).**

For Tokyo area, sunset time will be at **17:54**. Hilal may be seen starting 5 minutes before it and until 16 minutes after it. For other areas of Japan, sunset time can be checked from the internet.

Please inform the Committee if the Hilal is sighted or not by the form, according to Shariah evidence.

## Online Meeting

After collecting information from all over Japan and Malaysia Authorities for Hilal, R.H.C.J. will decide about 1st Shawwal (Eid Al Fitr) 1447 A.H. and announce it around **20:50** In Shaa Allah.
