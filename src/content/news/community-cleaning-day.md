Assalamu'alaikum warahmatullahi wabarakatuh,

We are calling all community members to join our monthly **Community Mosque Cleaning Day**. Keeping the mosque clean is a form of worship, and it is a collective responsibility that brings our community closer together.


## What We Will Do

During the cleaning day, we will:

- Clean and mop the main prayer hall
- Clean and organize the wudu (ablution) area
- Tidy up the entrance and shoe racks
- Clean windows and doors
- Organize the mosque library
- General maintenance check

## What to Bring

- Work clothes (comfortable, modest)
- Cleaning gloves (optional, we have extras)
- Your energy and positive spirit!

Cleaning supplies will be provided by the mosque.

*Mosque Committee*
